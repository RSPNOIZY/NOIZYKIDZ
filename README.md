# NOIZYKIDZ

**Haptic music education. Deaf-first. Autism-calm. Every kid plays.**

NOIZYKIDZ is a music education platform built on the principle that sound is not the only door into music. Vibration, pattern, colour, and touch are equally valid entry points — and for many children, they are the *primary* ones.

## Design Principles

- **Deaf-first design** — If it doesn't work without hearing, it doesn't ship.
- **Autism-calm** — No sensory ambush. Every interaction is predictable, adjustable, and opt-in.
- **Haptic-native** — Rhythm lives in the body before it lives in the ear.
- **Consent-native** — Children's data, likeness, and creative output are protected by the same standards we apply to adults. No exceptions.

## What Lives Here

Code, documentation, design specs, and educational frameworks.

No audio. No video. Media assets live on THE AQUARIUM and are never committed to version control.
